

# Digital Skills & Professional Readiness.

## Description
This repository contains a collection of practical assignments completed as part of my academic coursework. The project focuses on building essential digital, professional, and productivity skills required for students entering the technology and corporate environment.
The practicals cover areas such as resume design, professional communication, presentations, digital awareness, Google tools, Excel data analysis, and introductory use of AI tools.

## Objectives
- Develop basic professional and digital literacy
- Learn practical usage of tools like Canva, Google Forms, Excel, LinkedIn, and AI platforms
- Understand concepts of digital safety, data handling, and professional communication
- Build a documented record of hands-on learning

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Canva
- Google Docs, Google Forms, Google Drive
- MS Excel
- PowerPoint / Google Slides
- LinkedIn
- AI tools (ChatGPT, Gemini)

## Contents
- Resume Design
- Career-Oriented Presentation
- Digital Awareness Poster
- Professional Email Draft
- Google Forms (Attendance, Quiz)
- Excel Projects (Results, Sales Data, Financial Planner)
- AI Bias & Writing Comparison
- Cloud Storage & Digital Identity Tasks

## Note

This project is academic and beginner-level, created to demonstrate learning and understanding rather than advanced development.
